Kurukshetra is a historical city in the northern Indian state of Haryana. It is believed to be the site of the ancient city of Kurukshetra, which was the setting for the Mahabharata, one of the two major Sanskrit epics of ancient India. The Mahabharata tells the story of the Kuru dynasty and the great battle that took place between the Kauravas and the Pandavas, two branches of the same family. According to Hindu tradition, the battle of Kurukshetra was fought on a massive scale, involving thousands of soldiers and lasting for 18 days. The city is also considered a sacred pilgrimage site for Hindus, and is home to several temples and other religious sites.
